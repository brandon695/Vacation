Clock inventory

Document controller hardware, programming details, and zone configuration so inspections stay consistent across technicians.

Clock identification

  • Make & model: searchable library of major manufacturers plus custom entries.
  • Installation location: specify building, yard area, enclosure type, and photos.
  • Controller access: track keys, lock codes, Wi-Fi credentials, or cellular modem info.
  • Power & sensors: note power source, master valve, rain sensor, flow sensor, and pump controls.

Programming snapshot

  • Store zone count, station labeling, run times, start times, and seasonal adjust percentages.
  • Record watering restrictions (days allowed, time windows) to avoid compliance issues.
  • Log firmware version, connectivity status, and last sync date for smart controllers.
  • Attach export files from compatible smart controllers for audit history.

Zone catalog

Zones inherit defaults from the clock but can be customized with plant material, precipitation rate, head type, and gallons per minute. Track meter readings, flush dates, and notes that surface automatically when a zone is inspected.

Maintenance history

  • Keep a running log of repairs, parts replaced, and warranty expiration dates.
  • Upload invoices or manufacturer manuals for quick reference.
  • Flag clocks that need upgrades or conversions (e.g., 2-wire to decoder systems).
  • Track communication issues for remote-monitoring controllers.

Inspection integration

  • Start inspections directly from the clock profile with pre-filled zone lists.
  • Highlight priority zones based on previous issues or high water use.
  • Generate printable clock summaries for field binders.
  • Sync clock changes back to the property record automatically.
