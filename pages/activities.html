Activities & Key Details

National Park Duck Tours

  • ~75 min amphibious tour; Bathhouse Row + Lake Hamilton
  • Pricing: Adults $30; Children (3–12) $20 (free duck quacker)
  • Times (Fri–Sun): 9:30, 11:00, 12:30, 2:00, 4:00, 6:00, 7:30 (every 15 min when busy)
  • Times (Mon–Thu): 11:00, 12:30, 2:00, 4:00, 6:00, 7:30 (every 15 min when busy)
  • USCG regulated: every guest counted (lap infants too)
  • 418 Central Ave • 501‑520‑1242

Hot Springs Mountain Tower

  • 216‑ft elevator to observation decks (1,256 ft ASL); up to 140‑mile views
  • Hours: 9:00 a.m. – 8:00 p.m. (final tickets 7:30 p.m. Spring–Fall; 4:30 Winter)
  • Admission: Adult $13; Senior $12; First Responders $12; Park Pass $11; Youth $6.50; 4 & under free; Group Adult $7 / Child $5
  • 401 Hot Springs Mountain Dr • 501‑881‑4020

Ron Coleman Mining (Dig & Tours)

  • Dig your own crystals; fresh tailings often.
  • Dig hours: 8:00 a.m. – 5:30 p.m. (no permits within 60 mins of close)
  • Dig prices: Adults 16+ $25; Seniors 55+ $20; Children 7–15 $5; 6 & under free
  • Tours: 9:00, 10:00, 11:00, 12:00, 1:30, 2:30, 3:30 (45–60 min)
  • Tour prices: Tour only: Adult $15 / Kid $10; Tour+Dig: Adult $35 / Kid $12.50
  • 211 Crystal Ridge Ln, Jessieville • 501‑984‑5396

Blanchard Springs Caverns (Dripstone)

  • Guided cave tour (paved); cool temps year-round
  • Plan 1–1.5 hr tour + 2h36m drive each way
  • 704 Blanchard Springs Rd, Fifty‑Six • 870‑757‑2211

Horseback Riding – Lake Catherine Stables

  • Guided rides; seasonal. Reservations recommended
  • Approx 1 hour ride; arrive 15–20 min early
  • Inside Lake Catherine SP • 501‑262‑0777

Spa Day – Quapaw & Buckstaff

  • Quapaw: modern thermal pools; Buckstaff: traditional services
  • Quapaw • 201 Central Ave • 501‑620‑6714
    Buckstaff • 369 Central Ave • 501‑620‑6714
