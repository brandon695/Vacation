Support & troubleshooting

Get help from our team or find quick answers to keep inspections running smoothly.

Contact us

  • Support hours: Monday–Friday, 6 a.m.–6 p.m. PT
  • Phone: 800‑555‑1234
  • Email: support@irrigationmanager.app
  • Emergency after-hours: leave a voicemail for the on-call specialist.

Submit a ticket

  • Include customer/property link, clock, and zone numbers when reporting an issue.
  • Attach screenshots or exported reports to speed up diagnosis.
  • We respond within 1 business hour for critical outages and 4 hours for standard requests.

Common topics

  • Inspection form customization
  • Bulk importing customers and properties
  • Integrations with smart controllers and field service software
  • Resetting technician login or MFA

System status

Check status.irrigationmanager.app for live uptime details and maintenance windows. Subscribe to updates via email or SMS.

Self-help tips

  • Use the in-app keyboard shortcuts panel (press ?) to speed up data entry.
  • Offline mode caches the last 30 days of inspections per technician device.
  • Export sample data from the sandbox environment before going live.
