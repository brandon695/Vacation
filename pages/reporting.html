Reporting & insights

Transform inspection data into customer-ready deliverables and internal dashboards.

Customer reports

  • Generate branded PDF summaries with photos, issue descriptions, and recommended repairs.
  • Include cost estimates by zone and priority level to streamline approvals.
  • Highlight water savings opportunities, seasonal adjustments, and compliance risks.
  • Email reports automatically or share via customer portal with acknowledgment tracking.

Internal dashboards

  • Monitor inspection completion rates, technician productivity, and outstanding issues.
  • Visualize issue categories (leaks, coverage, electrical) to inform training and inventory.
  • Track water usage trends when paired with flow sensor readings.
  • Export data to CSV, QuickBooks, or business intelligence tools.

Key metrics

Resolution time
Average days from inspection to completed repair.
Water variance
Compare planned vs. actual usage to target leaks.
Zone reliability
Identify zones with repeated issues to prioritize retrofits.

Distribution options

  • Schedule recurring monthly or quarterly summary emails to stakeholders.
  • Provide secure share links that expire after a set period.
  • Allow customers to request repairs directly from the report.
  • Log when reports are viewed or downloaded for compliance.

Integration touchpoints

  • Sync completed inspections to field service or CMMS platforms.
  • Push issue data to task management tools for repair tracking.
  • Trigger invoice drafts with labor and material totals.
  • Archive reports to cloud storage with retention rules.
