Customer records

Store contact information, service preferences, and linked properties for every client you manage.

Core fields

  • Name: formal account name plus optional site contact.
  • Phone & email: validated formats with click-to-call and mail actions.
  • Preferred contact window: add weekday availability, language needs, or text messaging consent.
  • Linked properties: connect one or many service locations, each with billing or service notes.

Account management tools

  • Tag customers by contract type (residential, HOA, commercial) for filtered scheduling.
  • Track renewal dates, service level agreements, and assigned account manager.
  • Upload documents such as signed proposals or water agency permits.
  • Maintain billing preferences including invoice recipients and payment terms.

Customer view layout

Profile panel

Displays key contact info, emergency details, and quick links to schedule inspections or send reminders. Pin notes that should surface on every technician work order.

Property rollup

Shows each linked property with status indicators (last inspection, open issues, pending quotes). Technicians can jump into property details or start new inspections directly.
