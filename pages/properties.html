Property management

Capture service locations, special access instructions, and attach one or more irrigation clocks to each property.

Property profile

  • Address & geo: structured street, city, state, ZIP, and GPS coordinates for routing.
  • Customer link: select the owning customer plus on-site contacts or tenants.
  • Site notes: include gate codes, water source details, or landscape highlights.
  • Service history: automatically shows last inspection date, outstanding issues, and open work orders.

Attachments & tags

  • Upload property maps, planting plans, or irrigation as-builts.
  • Tag by property type (residential estate, HOA common area, commercial campus).
  • Store compliance requirements like backflow certifications or drought restrictions.
  • Track irrigation water source (municipal, well, reclaimed) for reporting.

Clock roster

Each property displays its installed clocks with make, model, location on-site, and quick indicators for connectivity, master valve, flow sensor, and total zones. From here you can launch a new inspection or review the last technician's notes.

Checklist before inspections

  • Confirm site access details and after-hours approvals.
  • Review recent issues so technicians focus on problem zones.
  • Check if clocks were recently reprogrammed to avoid overwriting changes.
  • Sync property photos to offline devices when heading to remote locations.

Smart scheduling cues

  • Use auto-generated seasonal reminders based on last inspection.
  • Group nearby properties for route optimization and technician workload balancing.
  • Filter by property tags (e.g., "water district audit due") to plan compliance visits.
  • Display weather-related holds for rain delays or freeze protection.
